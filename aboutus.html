Our History

Founded: 1963

CEO: Marco Lemagele

We are a Family Owned Organic Foods Company

Bountiful Foods originates from a rich traditional family drink that has been passed down from one generation to another. The drinks and fruits have been tasted and found fit for human consumption by the FDA. The company thought was born after friends tasted our family made drinks during our great-grandparent's wedding anniversary. Our drinks have become better and better with time as we believe in providing quality and healthy fruits and drinks. In 2010, we expanded from covering just Carlsbad to many other towns and beaches in California. Due to demand for specialized drinks that are home delivered, we launched our customize for yourself drinks in 2013.
